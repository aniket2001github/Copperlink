Advanced Copper Coils for Split AC Systems by Copper Link International

Copper Link International introduces state-of-the-art Copper Coils meticulously designed for Split AC systems, revolutionizing the essential components required in air conditioning and refrigerant systems. Serving as a vital conduit for refrigerants, these coils ensure seamless flow between system components while containing the refrigerant, preventing any escape into the atmosphere. Tailored for HVACR applications, Copper Link International's Copper Coils elevate the performance and longevity of Split AC systems, setting a new standard in air conditioning technology.

Key Features:

  • Anti-Corrosive Excellence
  • Soft (Annealed) Temper
  • Diverse Dimensions:
  • Efficient Thermal Conductivity
  • Low Pressure Loss and High Mechanical Stability
  • Environmental and Aesthetic Appeal

Anti-Corrosive Excellence

When compared to aluminium, copper coils made of C12200 - (DHP) High Phosphorus Deoxidized Tough Pitch Copper have better anti-corrosive qualities. The longevity of air conditioning systems is increased by their resistance to oxidation and corrosion.

Soft (Annealed) Temper

Copper Link International's coils are made with a soft (annealed) temper and are pliable and simple to install, which guarantees a smooth transition into HVACR systems.

Diverse Dimensions

These coils meet a variety of system needs with a minimum thickness of 0.508mm (0.020") and a range of exterior diameters from (3/16") 4.76mm to (7/8") 22.22mm. Options for coil lengths range from 15 to 100 metres.

Efficient Thermal Conductivity

Because of its natural thermal conductivity, copper helps air conditioning systems operate more efficiently and provide the best possible heat transmission.

Low Pressure Loss and High Mechanical Stability

The coils exhibit bactericidal, fungicidal, and algicidal effects, contributing to a cleaner and healthier air conditioning environment.

Environmental and Aesthetic Appeal

Beyond functionality, Copper Link International's coils are not only aesthetically pleasing but also environmentally conscious, being recyclable and sustainable.

  • Why choose Copper Coils over other materials for HVACR systems?
    Copper Coils, specifically C12200 copper, offer superior anti-corrosive properties, excellent thermal conductivity, and resistance to oxidation, ensuring a longer lifespan and optimal performance for air conditioning systems.
  • What are the installation advantages of Copper Link International's Copper Coils?
    The coils are soft (annealed), making them easy to install and flexible for seamless integration into HVACR systems.
