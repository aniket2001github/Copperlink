Premium Medical Grade Copper Tubing: Unmatched Quality and Performance

Copper Link International’s pressurized ACR/Med copper tubing is available in 10,12, and 20 ft. lengths, and a variety of sizes, both Type L and Type K.

Each product conforms to ASTM B-280/B-819 specifications, is specially cleaned, de-burred, purged and pressurized with nitrogen gas, and features easy to remove, installer friendly, rubber plugs.

Copper Link International uses modern and environment friendly aqueous degreasing system to achieve the ASTM B 280 cleanliness limit of less than 0.0035 g/sq. ft on interior surfaces.

Frequently Asked Question

  • What are the advantages of using medical-grade copper tubing in healthcare applications?
    Medical-grade copper tubing offers excellent antimicrobial properties, reducing the risk of bacterial transmission. It is durable, corrosion-resistant, and easy to clean, making it a safe and reliable choice for critical healthcare environments.
  • How does Copper Link International ensure the quality and cleanliness of their medical-grade copper tubing?
    Copper Link International adheres to strict ASTM B-280/B-819 specifications. Our tubing undergoes specialized cleaning, thorough de-burring, and is pressurized with nitrogen gas to ensure purity. This process guarantees tubing with less than 0.0035 g/sq. ft of residue on interior surfaces.
  • Can I get custom lengths or sizes of medical-grade copper tubing for specific healthcare projects?
    Yes, Copper Link International can accommodate custom orders. Contact our customer service team to discuss your specific requirements, and we'll work with you to provide the tailored solution you need for your healthcare applications.
